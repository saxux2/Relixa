# End-User Documentation

Welcome to Relixa! Depending on your role, consult the following flows:

## Organizers
- Log in and establish campaigns.
- Issue specific fund requests and attach certified beneficiaries.

## Donors
- Navigate available disaster campaigns.
- Submit XLM natively into secure escrows knowing exactly where it's going via the Dashboard.

## Beneficiaries & Merchants
- Receive your token allowances directly to your decentralized wallet.
- Spend specifically within the merchant ecosystem directly tied to the smart contracts logic constraints.
